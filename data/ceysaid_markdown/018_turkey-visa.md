# Turkey Visa

## **Required Documents for Turkey Visa Application**

Applicants must submit the following documents when applying for a Turkey visa:

1. **Passport & Copies**
   * A valid passport with at least **six months of validity** beyond the intended stay.
   * A photocopy of the passport’s biographical page.
   * All previous passports (if applicable).
   * If a passport is lost, a **police report translated into English** must be submitted.
2. **Visa Application Form**
   * A **duly filled** and signed visa application form.
3. **Photographs**
   * **Two recent biometric photographs** (5×6 cm) with a **white background**.
   * The uploaded and submitted photographs must be the **same**.
4. **Invitation Letter (Optional)**
   * If applicable, an invitation letter from Türkiye.
   * The invitation letter should be attested by a **notary public in Türkiye**.
5. **Financial & Employment Documents**
   * Documents proving the financial status of both the applicant and the inviting person.
   * **Official letter from employer/school/university** confirming leave approval.
   * A **signed letter from the applicant** explaining the purpose of the visit.
   * **Salary slips or confirmation letter of salary** for the past three months.
   * **Bank statements for the last six months** showing financial stability.
6. **Travel & Accommodation Details**
   * **Flight reservation** (round-trip).
   * **Hotel booking or invitation letter** from a host in Türkiye.
   * The invitation letter should be **notarized** in Türkiye.
7. **Additional Supporting Documents**
   * Proof of **commitment to return**, such as a **land registry, business certificate, salary table, or bank account statement**.
   * A **No Objection Letter (NOC) or affidavit** from the non-accompanying parent(s) for children under 18, attested by the **Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Sri Lanka**.
   * **Marriage certificate and birth certificates of children** for family applications.

## **Processing Time & Fees**

* The visa processing time ranges from **15 days up to 2 months**, depending on the application and embassy approval.
* The visa **fee is non-refundable** under any circumstances.

## **Visa Requirements for Sri Lankan Nationals**

* **Official and ordinary passport holders** are required to obtain a visa before entering Türkiye.
* **Sri Lankan nationals** holding a valid **Schengen, USA, UK, or Ireland visa or residence permit** may be eligible for an **e-Visa**.
