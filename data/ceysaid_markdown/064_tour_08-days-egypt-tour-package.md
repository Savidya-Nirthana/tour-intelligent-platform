## 08 Days Egypt Tour Package

**SEND US A WHATSAPP MESSAGE FOR BOOKING YOUR TOUR**

**Day 01 CAIRO**

Arrive Cairo International Airport meet and assist by our Tour Leader then drive you by air-conditioned private Car to your hotel in which you will be assisted by our Tour Leader for check-in. then, overnight in Cairo.

**Day 02CAIRO**

after Breakfast start city tour visit Egyptian Museum, lunch in local restaurant. After that Visit Pyramids of Giza and Sphinx Then Dinner, Overnight in hotel.

**Day 03 CAIRO**

After Breakfast start city tour visit Old Cairo, Churches,(However, it traditionally being with the visit of Holy family to Egypt ) and synagogue of Ben Isra. Then Lunch in local restaurant visit Citadel and the Alabaster Mosque of Mohamed Ali ,. Then transfer back Hotel Dinner ,Overnight in Hotel.

**Day 04 CAIRO/LUXOR**

After breakfast transfer to Apt. Flight to Luxor. Arrive Luxor then Meet & Assistance and transfer to Motor ship. Embarkation. Lunch. Panoramic Visit of Luxor Temples and visit Karnak temples. Dinner and Overnight on board .

**Day 05 LUXOR**

After Breakfast Visit King’s Valley,Then Visit Hatshepsut temple After that visit colossi of Memnon then Lunch , sail to Edfu. Full on board, Dinner overnight on board .

**Day 06 EDFU/KOM OMBO**

Arrive Edfu, After breakfast visit the temple. Then Lunch After that Sail to Kom Ombo. Arrive &amp; visit Kom Ombo temple.Dinner and Overnight on board. Sail to Aswan .Overnight  in board  
**Day 07 ASWAN**

Arrive Aswan after breakfast visits High Dam, Old Dam, Then visit Philae temple. Felucca ride passing by botanical garden Full board and Overnight on board.

**Day 08 ASWAN/CAIRO**

After breakfast , disembarkation Flight back to Cairo Apt connexion for final departure. End of services.
