# Nepal Visa

### **Visa for First-Time Visitors**

* **FREE Visa on Arrival** for **first-time visitors** to Nepal.

### **Visa for Second-Time Visitors**

* A **visa fee of USD 30+** applies for **second-time visitors**.

### **Important Travel Requirement**

* If your flight to Nepal is routed **through India**, you must obtain an **Indian Entry Visa** in advance.

### **General Terms & Conditions**

* **Visa fees are non-refundable**, even if the visa is rejected.
* Entry requirements and visa policies are subject to change. Travelers should check with the relevant authorities before departure.
