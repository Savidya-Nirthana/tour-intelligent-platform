**Day 2: 15 SEP (B/L/D)**  
Istanbul  
After a delicious breakfast you will leave for the famous Princes’ Islands, which is one of the favourite local spots to beat the monotony of city life and venturing into an entirely different, vibrant atmosphere. The lavishly decorated Ottoman mansions and  
Victorian themed villas will leave you spellbound. After you have explored the island, enjoy a delectable lunch at a seaside restaurant. You will return from the hotel via ferry and will be transferred back to the hotel.  
Lunch at local restaurant  
Dinner at local restaurant  
Overnight in Istanbul.

**Day 3: 16 SEP (B/L/D)**  
Istanbul – Two Continents Tour  
Today after breakfast proceed for full Day Two Continents Tour. Start the day by visiting Beylerbeyi Palace ( Asia side ), recent residence for Ottoman Sultans. Next will visit Camlıca Hill where you can enjoy full view of the city . then continue to visit Camlıca  
Mosque which is the most big Mosque build in Latestly. Then Enjoy the Bosphorus Cruise ride (sharing) with the straits of both Europe and Asia with a possibility to photograph the unique beauties of the Bosphorus.

Rumeli Fortress (from the boat-no interior visit) historical armament built by the Conqueror to control and protect the Bosphorus. Golden Horn, the arm of the Bosphorus and a natural harbour of the historical peninsula. Byzantine City Walls, strongest fortification of the middle age. End the day by visiting Spice Bazaar (Egyptian Bazaar), mystical smells of different aromas of various spices. Shops selling fruits, vegetables, fish, flowers etc.  
Lunch at local restaurant  
Dinner at local restaurant  
Overnight in Istanbul.

**Day 4: 17 SEP (B/L/D)**  
Istanbul  
We will explore this beautiful city passing by Istanbul City Walls, massive walls of Ancient Constantinople. Then visit the Blue Mosque which boasts a unique feature of 6 minarets. Then, proceed to the only remains of Hippodrome of Constantinople, which  
consists of 3 monumental columns and a fountain. Visit Topkapi Palace, it is a museum palace with a wide collection of porcelain, antiques, holy relics and other treasures.  
Next proceed to Hagia Sophia (from outside), once a church but converted into a mosque, one of the greatest marvels of architecture. The end of tour, we proceed to the Grand Bazaar for shopping.  
Lunch at local restaurant  
Dinner at local restaurant  
Overnight in Istanbul.

**Day 5: 18 SEP (B/D)**  
Istanbul / Pamukkale

Lunch not include because of flight  
Dinner at Hotel

**Day 6: 19 SEP (B/L/D)**  
Pamukkale  
Breakfast at hotel. Today you will discover Hierapolis. Drive to Pamukkale that has the meaning of the Cotton Castle in English. Once known as Hierapolis, famous for healing thermal / mineral Baths and beautiful snow-white “petrified waterfalls. After lunch visit  
Hierapolis, including the magnificently preserved Necropolis, the main Street and Gates, the thermal Baths, the devil’s hole, and Masterpiece Theater. Pamukkale terraces are made of travertine, a sedimentary rock deposited by water from the hot springs.

In this area, there are 17 hot water springs in which the temperature ranges from 35 °C to 100 °C The water that emerges from the spring is transported 320 meters (1,050 ft.) to the head of the travertine terraces and deposits calcium carbonate on a section 60 to 70 meters long covering an expanse of 240 meters to 300 meters.  
Swimming or a therapeutic bath in a hotel pool’s hot spring mineral Waters.  
Lunch at local restaurant  
Dinner at Hotel  
Overnight in Pamukkale.

Dinner at Hotel
