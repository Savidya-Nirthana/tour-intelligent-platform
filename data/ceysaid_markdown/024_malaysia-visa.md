# Malaysia Visa

### **Visa Requirements for Travelers**

To apply for a **Malaysia Visa**, applicants must provide the following documents:

1. **Passport Requirements**
   * Passport must be valid for **at least seven (7) months** from the expected date of arrival in Malaysia.
2. **Photograph**
   * **Recent passport-size photo** with a **white background**.
3. **Passport Copy**
   * A **clear scanned copy** of the **passport bio-data page**.
4. **Previous Travel History**
   * Details of previous international travel, if applicable.
5. **Return Flight Ticket**
   * A confirmed **return ticket** (Maximum stay: **30 days in Malaysia**).
6. **Hotel Booking Confirmation**
   * Proof of **accommodation booking** for the stay in Malaysia.
7. **Financial Proof**
   * **Latest 3-month bank statement** & a **balance confirmation letter**.
   * **First-time travelers** must have a minimum bank balance of **$500**, verified with a **bank confirmation letter**.
8. **Visa Request Letter**
   * A **typed and signed letter** from the applicant requesting the visa.
9. **Employment or Business Documentation**
   * **Employment Letter** from the employer **OR**
   * **Business Registration Document** (Sinhala & English Translation) for business owners.
10. **Marriage Certificate** (If applicable)

* Must be translated into **Sinhala and English**.

11. **For Applicants Below 12 Years Old**

* **Birth Certificate** (Sinhala & English Translation) is **mandatory**.

### **Processing Time**

* **Standard Processing:** **2 working days**.
* If additional documents are requested by the visa officer, processing may be delayed by **2 more days**.
* Applicants must **submit any requested documents within 5 days**; failure to do so may result in visa rejection.

### **Important Notes**

* **Visa fees are non-refundable**, even if the application is rejected.
* **eVISA applications must be submitted from outside Malaysia** (except for applicants from Israel & North Korea, who are not eligible).
* If you are from a **non-eVISA eligible country**, you must **apply at the nearest Malaysian High Commission, Embassy, Consulate General, or Malaysia Visa Center**.
