# Philippine Visa

## **Required Documents for Philippines Visa Application**

Applicants must submit the following documents for a **Philippines visa**:

### **1. Visa Application Form**

* A **duly completed** visa application form.
* Must be filled out in **black or blue ink** using **BLOCK letters**.

### **2. Passport & Photocopies**

* A **passport valid for at least six (6) months** beyond the intended stay.
* **Photocopies** of the biometric page and any alteration pages.

### **3. Photographs**

* **Two recent colored photographs** (2″ x 2″) with a white background.

### **4. Proof of Financial Capacity**

**a. Bank Statements:**

* **Original bank statements** showing **substantial transactions for the last six (6) months**.
* **Certified by the bank** (E-statements must also be certified).
* If no statements are available, an **updated bank book (certified by the bank)** must be provided.
* Minimum balance requirement: **Rs. 300,000** or more, maintained consistently.
* Fixed deposits (certified by the bank) can be submitted as an optional document.
* **Consent letter for joint bank accounts** (if applicable).
* If **sponsored by an immediate family member**, proof of relationship is required (**birth or marriage certificate**, translated into English and certified by a notary public or JP).

**b. Business Income Proof (For Business Owners):**

* Copy of the **Business Registration Certificate**, certified by the **Divisional Secretariat**.

### **5. Travel & Accommodation Details**

* **Flight reservations** (entry & exit from the Philippines).
* **Hotel bookings or proof of lodging** for the duration of stay.

### **6. Sponsorship & Company Support (If Applicable)**

* **For incentive tours sponsored by a company:**
  + A sponsorship letter signed by **two directors OR one director & a senior manager**.
  + Certified copy of the **Business Registration Certificate**.
  + **List of directors**, certified by the **company secretary**.
  + If the applicant is a director, another director and a senior manager must sign.
* **If sponsored from the Philippines:**
  + **Letter of invitation**, authenticated by a **notary public in the Philippines**.
  + Sponsor’s **passport copy**.
  + Sponsor’s **certified bank statements for the last six (6) months**.

### **7. Employment & Ties to Home Country**

* **Letter from employer** granting leave and confirming return to Sri Lanka.
* If self-employed, a **personal letter explaining the trip**.
* Documents proving **ties to the home country**, such as:
  + **Employment contract or letter**.
  + **Property ownership documents** (if requested).
  + **Sri Lankan residence visa** (for foreign passport holders residing in Sri Lanka).

### **8. Police Clearance (If Requested)**

* The **Philippine Consulate may require a police clearance** at a later stage.

## **Processing Time & Visa Validity**

* **Processing Time:** **10-15 working days** (subject to DFA approval).
* **Single-entry visa** valid for **3 months**.

---

## **Additional Requirements for Minor Applicants (Sri Lankans)**

* **Birth certificate** (Sinhala & English translation) – *Original required at submission, or a certified copy by a JP/notary public*.
* **Marriage certificate of parents** (English translation).
* **Letter from parents** confirming that the child is accompanied by them, along with their passport copies.
* If parents are **not accompanying the child**, a **notarized consent letter** from both parents is required.
* **For students:** A letter from the **school/university** mentioning the passport number and granting permission to travel.

## **General Instructions for All Visa Applications**

![✔️](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/2714.svg) All relevant letters must be addressed to the **Consulate General of the Republic of the Philippines, Colombo**, mentioning the correct address and the applicant’s passport details.

![✔️](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/2714.svg) The **Consulate may request additional documents** at its discretion.

![✔️](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/2714.svg) Submission of complete documents **does not guarantee visa approval**.

![✔️](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/2714.svg) Applications missing any required documents **will not be processed**. The decision of the **Processing Officer is final**.

![✔️](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/2714.svg) **Group Applications (5 or more applicants)** must be submitted **before 11:00 AM**.

![✔️](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/2714.svg) **Visa Fee:** The applicable visa fee is payable in **Sri Lankan Rupees** and is **subject to change without notice**. **Visa fees are non-refundable**.

![✔️](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/2714.svg) **Documents submitted (original or copies) will NOT be returned.** Applicants should carry original documents for verification during the interview.

![⚠️](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/26a0.svg) **DO NOT finalize travel bookings until your visa is approved.** A **temporary flight itinerary** issued by an approved travel agent can be submitted instead.

For further inquiries, feel free to contact our visa support team. Safe travels!
