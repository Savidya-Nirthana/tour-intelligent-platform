# Uzbekistan

## **Required Documents for Uzbekistan Visa Application**

To apply for a visa to Uzbekistan, applicants must provide the following documents:

1. **White Background Photo**
2. **Passport Bio Page**
3. **Place of Work and Position**
4. **Permanent Address**
5. **Preliminary Place of Residence in Uzbekistan**

## **Visa Processing Time**

* The standard processing time for a Uzbekistan visa is **3 working days**.
* Processing times may vary based on application accuracy and additional requirements.

### **Important Notes:**

* All information provided must be accurate and complete to avoid delays in processing.
* Visa regulations and requirements may vary depending on nationality.
* Additional supporting documents may be requested by the authorities.
