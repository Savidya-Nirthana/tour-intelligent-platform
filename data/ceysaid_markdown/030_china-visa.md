# China Visa

#### **China Visa Required Documents:**

1. **Original Passport**
   * Your passport must have at least **six months of remaining validity** and **blank visa pages**.
   * Provide a **photocopy of the passport’s data page** and the **photo page** if it is separate.
   * If you have a previous passport, please include it.
2. **Photograph**
   * A **white background photo** (soft copy) sized **33mm × 48mm**.
3. **Bank Statements**
   * A **copy of your latest month’s personal bank statement** (1 month) along with a **Balance Confirmation Letter** addressed to the **China Embassy**.
   * The balance should demonstrate at least **100 USD per day** during your stay in China.
   * **Fixed deposits** are **not accepted**.
4. **Air Tickets**
5. **Hotel Reservation**
6. **Leave Letter**
7. **Request Letter**
8. **Guest Number**

#### **China Visa Application Form Questions:**

1. Occupation
2. Employment start date (If retired, use the end date)
3. Employer’s name
4. Employer’s address
5. Employer’s telephone number
6. Your position at work
7. Job duties
8. Supervisor’s name
9. Supervisor’s telephone number
10. Highest level of education
11. Name of school/university
12. Diploma/Degree obtained
13. Major (the subject focus of your studies)
14. Resident address
15. Applicant’s email ID
16. Family Members:
    * Spouse Name and date of birth
    * Spouse Occupation
    * Father’s Name and date of birth
    * Mother’s Name and date of birth
    * Children’s Names and dates of birth
    * For children who are students, provide **grade and school name**

#### **Processing Time**:

* **Normal Visa**: **4 Working Days**
* **Urgent Visa**: **3 Working Days** (Additional charges from the embassy: + Visa Fee)

#### **Additional Requirement**:

If you have previously visited **Turkey** or **Pakistan**, you must provide proof of the visit (i.e., air ticket, itinerary, and tour plan).

---

Please make sure to submit all the required documents in the prescribed format to avoid delays in processing.
