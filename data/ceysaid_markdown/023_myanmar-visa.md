# Myanmar Visa

### **Visa Requirements for Travelers**

To apply for a Myanmar visa, ensure you meet the following requirements:

1. **Passport Validity**
   * Your passport must be valid for **at least six (6) months** from the date of arrival in Myanmar.
2. **Photograph**
   * **One recent color photo** (4.6 cm x 3.8 cm) taken within the **last three (3) months**.
3. **Passport Bio-Data Page**
   * A **clear scanned copy** of your **passport bio-data page** must be uploaded.
4. **Proof of Return Ticket**
   * A confirmed **return ticket** from Myanmar must be attached to the application.
5. **Hotel Booking Confirmation**
   * Travelers must provide **proof of hotel booking** for their stay.
   * **Tourists must stay in registered hotels, motels, inns, guesthouses, or resorts** during their visit.

### **Processing Time**

* The minimum processing time for a **Tourist e-Visa (T)** is **three (3) working days** (Myanmar time).

### **Entry Points for eVisa Holders**

Currently, passengers with an **approved eVisa** are permitted to enter Myanmar **only through** the following international entry points:

![✈️](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/2708.svg) **Airports:**

* Yangon International Airport
* Mandalay International Airport
* Nay Pyi Taw International Airport

![🚪](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/1f6aa.svg) **Land Border Checkpoint:**

* Kawthaung International Land Border Checkpoint

### **Important Notes**

* **Visa fees are non-refundable**, even if the application is rejected.
* Travelers should ensure they meet all entry requirements before applying.
