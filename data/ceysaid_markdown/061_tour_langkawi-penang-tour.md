Oriental Village Langkawi is the home of Langkawi Cable Car (SkyCab), which transports you 2,326 feet (709 meters) to the SkyBridge at the summit of Mt. Mat Cincang. LGK Island Hopping  
Gear up to explore some of the famous islands around Langkawi like Pulau Dayang Bunting, Pulau Singa Besar & and Pulau Beras Basah by a speedboat. Get picked up from your hotel and reach the meeting point at Jetty Teluk Baru. Get on the local speedboat and enjoy an amazing journey to the beautiful islands. Take a stop at Dayang Bunting Island and enjoy various activities at the Lake of the Pregnant Maiden (at additional charges). Head towards the Singa Besar island and see various wild eagles roaming around. Visit the Beras Basah island and spend your leisure time on the sand beach. Return to the harbor and end your trip with lots of amazing memories to cherish. Book the Langkawi Island Hopping Speedboat Tour and enjoy a scenic journey to the famous islands.
