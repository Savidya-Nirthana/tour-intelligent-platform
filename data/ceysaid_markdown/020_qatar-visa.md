# Qatar Visa

## **Required Documents for Qatar Visa Application**

Applicants must submit the following documents for a **Qatar tourist visa**:

1. **White Background Colored Scanned Photo**
   * A **high-quality scanned copy** of a recent **passport-sized color photo** with a white background.
   * **Mobile phone photos are NOT accepted** – only scanned copies will be processed.
2. **Passport Data Page**
   * A **colored scanned copy** of the passport’s **biographical page**.
   * The passport must be valid for **at least six months** from the travel date.
3. **Travel & Accommodation Details** *(Provided by Us)*
   * **Hotel voucher** (arranged by us).
   * **Airline ticket itinerary** (arranged by us).
4. **Visa Rejection History**
   * Applicants must provide information **if they have ever had a visa rejected** by any country.
5. **Family & Marital Status Documents**
   * **Married couples** must submit a **marriage certificate**.
   * **Families with children** must provide **birth certificates**.
   * **Single travelers** may face stricter visa approval conditions.
6. **Additional Documents**
   * **Qatar immigration may request additional documents**, and applicants will be informed accordingly.

## **Document Naming Format**

* All files must be **renamed with the applicant’s name** for faster processing.
* Example:
  + *John Passport, John Form 14, John Photo, John Ticket*

## **Visa Processing & Terms**

* **Processing Time:** **8-10 working days**
* **Visa Approval is NOT Guaranteed.**
* **No rejection reason or documents will be provided by Qatar immigration.**
* **Full package booking (hotel + flight) is required** once the visa is approved.
* **Visa fees are non-refundable** even if the visa is rejected.
