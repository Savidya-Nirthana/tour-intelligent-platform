# Vietnam Visa

## **Required Documents for Vietnam Visa Application**

To apply for a Vietnam visa, applicants must provide the following documents:

1. **White Background Photo**
2. **Passport Copy**
3. **Hotel Reservation**
4. **Air Ticket**
5. **Travel Itinerary**

## **Urgent Visa Processing**

For travelers who need a **rush visa**, an urgent visa letter can be processed. After receiving the approved visa letter, travelers will need to pay the visa stamping fee and provide a visa photograph upon arrival at the airport in Vietnam.

### **Important Notes:**

* The visa fee is **non-refundable** under any circumstances.
* Make sure all documents are accurate and complete to avoid processing delays.
* Additional requirements may apply based on nationality and travel history.
