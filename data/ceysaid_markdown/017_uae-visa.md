# UAE Visa

## **Required Documents for UAE Visa Application**

To apply for a UAE visa, applicants must submit the following documents:

1. **White Background Photo**
2. **Passport Copy**
3. **Hotel Reservation**
4. **Air Ticket**

## **Visa Processing Time**

* The standard processing period for a UAE visa is **4 working days**.
* Processing time may vary depending on **embassy approval** and additional requirements.

### **Important Notes:**

* The visa fee is **non-refundable** under any circumstances.
* Approval is subject to the UAE immigration authorities.
* Ensure all documents are complete and accurate to avoid delays.
* Additional documents may be requested based on nationality and travel history.
