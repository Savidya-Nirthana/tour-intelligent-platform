**Day 01: Arrive Hyderabad**  
Arrive Hyderabad, met at the airport and transfer to the hotel. Overnight: Hyderabad

**Day 02: Hyderabad**  
After breakfast at hotel morning proceeds for sightseeing tour of Charminar, Golconda Fort -The outer fort occupies an area of three square kilometers, which is 4.8 kilometers in length, the Royal Tombs of The Qutub Shahi Kings represent the most authentic and majestic display of the Qutub Shahi dynasty architectural traditions today, Birla Mandir in Hyderabad, dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara of Tirumala Tirupati Temple is said that a whopping quantity of white marble, which is almost 2000 tonnes, was brought from Rajasthan for building this temple, Nizam Museum is consisted of a wide range of rare souvenirs and exquisitely designed mementos, Chowmahalla Palace is one of the popular sightseeing places in Hyderabad. The literal meaning of this place is“Four Palaces” with “Chow” means four in Urdu and “Mahalat” which is the plural of “Mahalel” meaning palaces. Overnight: Hyderabad

**Day 03: Hyderabad – Departure**  
After breakfast, transfer to the airport to board the flight to onward journey.
