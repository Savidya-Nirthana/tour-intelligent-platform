# Egypt Visa

### **Egypt Tourist Visa Requirements**

#### **Required Documents:**

1. **All Documents Must Be Printed on One Side Only**
2. **Passport Validity**
   * Your passport must be valid for at least **6 months** or more from the date of application.
3. **Profession Details**
   * The applicant’s **profession** must be stated on the **bio page** of the passport or the **alteration page**.
4. **Passport Copy**
   * A **clear black & white copy** of the passport bio page.
   * If the profession is mentioned in the **alteration page**, provide a copy of that page as well.
   * Passport copies should be zoomed to fit on an **A4 sheet**.
5. **Photographs**
   * **Two colored photographs** with a white background, sized **4 x 6 cm**.
6. **Bank Statements**
   * **Personal bank statements** from the last **three months** (both copy and original).
7. **Employment/Business Documents**
   * If employed, a **service or employment letter**.
   * If an entrepreneur, a **copy of the business registration certificate**.
8. **Hotel Booking**
9. **Air Ticket**
10. **Tour Itinerary**

#### **Additional Requirements for Travel Agents:**

* **Letter from both agents (Sri Lankan & Egyptian)** indicating the **applicant’s names**, **passport numbers**, **professions**, and **passport expiry dates**.
* **Group Tours**: Ensure that the **order of the names** from both agents matches.
* **Names List**: The **names list of passengers** should be clear and well-spaced between rows for easy reading.

**Visa Processing Duration**

* Minimum processing time: **3 to 4 weeks**.
