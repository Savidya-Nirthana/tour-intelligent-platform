# Indian Visa

### **Required Documents for Visa Application**

1. **White Background Photo**
   * A **2 x 2-inch passport photo** with a **white background**.
2. **Passport Copy**
3. **Previous Indian Visa Copy**
4. **Home Address**
5. **Contact Number**
6. **Religion**
7. **Father’s Name**
8. **Father’s Place of Birth**
9. **Mother’s Name**
10. **Mother’s Place of Birth**

11. **Spouse Name (If married)**

12. **Spouse Place of Birth**

13. **Business Name/Employer Name**

14. **Business/Employer Address**

15. **Designation**

16. **Profession**

17. **Last Five Years Travel Countries Details**

18. **Previous Indian Resident Address**

19. **Invitation**

20. **Travel Date**

21. **Travel Airport in India**

22. **Departure Airport in India**

23. **Hotel Name or Accommodation Place**

24. **Reference in India**

### **Visa Categories and Duration**

* **e-Visa** Duration Options:
  + 30 days
  + 1 year

### **Visa Fees & Processing**

* **Processing Period**: Typically **4 working days**.
* **Sticker Visa**: Available upon request.
* **Urgent Visa**: Fees to be decided by the visa officer.

### **Special Notes**

* **Unemployed women/housewives/kids** must provide **father’s work details** or **spouse’s work details**.
* **Pakistan Visit**: If you have previously visited Pakistan, you must provide **proof of visit**.

### **Visa Types Available**

* **Tourist Visa**:
  + **1-month single entry**
  + **1-month double entry**
  + **3-month single entry**
  + **3-month double entry**
  + **6-month single entry**
  + **6-month double entry**
  + **6-month triple entry**
  + **6-month multiple entries**
  + **1-year multiple entries**

### **IVS Visa Locations**

* **Jaffna**
* **Colombo**
* **Kandy**

### **Important Note**

* **Visa Fee is non-refundable**, even if the application is rejected.
