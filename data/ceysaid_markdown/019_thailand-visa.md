# Thailand Visa

# **Thailand Visa for Sri Lankan Nationals**

## **Visa Options**

![✅](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/2705.svg) **Visa on Arrival (VOA) & E-Visa Available**  
Sri Lankan passport holders can apply for a **Visa on Arrival (VOA)** at designated entry points or apply for an **E-Visa** online before traveling to Thailand.

## **E-Visa Requirements**

To apply for a **Thailand E-Visa**, applicants must submit the following documents:

1. **Passport or Travel Document**
2. **Recent Photograph**
3. **Proof of Current Location**
4. **Contact Information**
5. **Travel & Accommodation Details**

## **Visa Types & Validity**

| **Visa Type** | **Number of Entries** | **Validity** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Single Entry Visa** | 1 Entry | Valid for **3 months** |
| **Multiple Entry Visa** | Multiple Entries | Valid for **6 months** |

## **Processing Time**

* The standard processing time for a **Thailand E-Visa** is **5 to 10 working days** after the submission of a complete application.
* Some applications may take longer depending on additional verification requirements by the Thai Embassy.

### **Important Notes:**

* The **visa fee is non-refundable** under any circumstances.
* Applicants must ensure all documents are accurate and complete to avoid delays.
