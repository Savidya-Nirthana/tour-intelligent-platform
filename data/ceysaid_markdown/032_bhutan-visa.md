# Bhutan Visa

#### **Bhutan Visa Required Documents:**

1. **Passport Copy**
   * A digital copy of your **passport**. The passport must be valid for **six months** from the intended date of departure from Bhutan.
2. **Photograph**
3. **Contact Information**
4. **Travel Dates**

#### **Visa Fee:**

* **Processing Time**: **5 Working Days**
* **Visa Fee**: **Non-refundable**

#### **Important Note:**

* If you are traveling to Bhutan with a flight allocated through **India**, you will need an **India Entry Visa** as well.

Ensure that all documents are submitted correctly to avoid delays in the visa application process.
