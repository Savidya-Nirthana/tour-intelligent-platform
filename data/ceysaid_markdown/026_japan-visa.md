# Japan Visa

### **Required Documents for Visa Application**

1. **Visa Application Form**
   * Each applicant, including children, must submit a separate form.
   * The form should be **filled in block letters** (typed or clearly handwritten).
   * The applicant’s name must match the **passport details**.
2. **Photograph**
   * **Size:** 2-inch x 1.4-inch (45mm x 35mm).
   * **Full-face view** on a **white or light-colored background**.
   * Taken within the **last 6 months**.
3. **Valid Passport**
   * Name and details should match the **National Identity Card (NIC)**.
   * **Two copies** of the **passport bio-data page**.
   * **One copy** of the **alteration page**, if applicable.
   * Must have at least **two blank pages**.
4. **Schedule of Stay**
   * A **detailed day-by-day itinerary** of activities in Japan.
5. **Accommodation Proof**
   * Hotel booking confirmation or a **sponsor’s residence certificate** if staying with a host.
6. **Travel Itinerary**
   * **Round-trip flight reservation** (It is recommended not to purchase tickets before visa approval).
7. **Employment & Financial Documents**
   * **Employed Applicants:**
     + A **letter from the employer** stating **employment start date** and **approved leave period**.
   * **Self-Employed Applicants:**
     + **Business registration document** (Original).
     + **Company Bank Statement** (Last 3 months, Original).
   * **Students:**
     + A **letter from the school or university** confirming the approved leave period.
8. **Personal Documents** (Mandatory)
   * **National Identity Card (NIC)**.
   * **Birth Certificate**.
   * **Marriage Certificate / Divorce Certificate / Death Certificate** (if applicable).
   * **Affidavit** (if there is a name mismatch in official documents).
9. **Personal Financial Records (Last 3 months)**
   * **Salary Slips**.
   * **Bank Statement & Bank Balance Confirmation Letter** (for current and savings accounts).
   * **Fixed Deposit** balance confirmation (if applicable).
10. **Previous Travel History to Japan (Last One Year)**

* Dates and duration of previous visits, if applicable.

### **Additional Notes**

* A **request letter** must be submitted individually by each applicant, stating they have applied through a travel agent.
* **Retired government employees** must submit **retirement documents**.
* **Non-government employees** should provide a **recommendation letter** explaining their employment status and source of funds.
* All **documents should be sorted** according to the checklist.
* **Original documents and A4-sized photocopies** must be submitted separately.
* **Documents in Sinhala or Tamil** must be accompanied by an **official English translation** by a sworn translator.
* **Do not use pins, staples, paper clips, or cello tape** on documents.
* The standard **processing time is 5 working days**, but additional scrutiny may extend this period.
* **Visa fees are non-refundable**, even if the application is rejected.
