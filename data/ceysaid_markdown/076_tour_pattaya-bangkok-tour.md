**DAY 02 Coral Island tour with Speed boat Hotel Breakfast + Lunch**  
Breakfast at the hotel  
Coral Island tour with join Speed boat with lunch  
An adventurous day with the surroundings that will turn your day into one of the most exciting and thrilling experiences of your life. Relax and travel to the mesmerizing and beautiful Koh Larn Coral Island, Pattaya, via a speedboat. On the beautiful islands with fantastic beaches, you can either have a day for leisure and rejuvenation or get the best of all the worlds by engaging in daunting and high-octane water sporting activities.  
Overnight in Pattaya

**DAY 03 Transfer to Bangkok + Dinner Cruise Hotel Breakfast + Dinner**  
After breakfast and check out at the hotel, Transfer to Bangkok  
Dinner Cruise  
Take a memorable cruise down the Chao Phraya in Bangkok and have an amazing time with your loved ones! While enjoying the buffet supper, taking in the live music show, and seeing the famous landmarks from the boat, you will get to experience Thailand's real culture and traditions. You will also enjoy a few of the sights at the city's many riverside attractions, such as the Grand Palace, the Rama VIII Bridge, and Wat Arun, which are all renowned worldwide. Enjoy your favorite cuisine in the elegant setting of the Chao Phraya Princess cruise and have a great time.  
Overnight in Bangkok

**DAY 04 Dream World Visa + Snow Town Hotel Breakfast + Lunch**  
Breakfast at the hotel  
Dream World Visa + Snow Town with Lunch  
Dream World is the absolute stuff for kids! You won’t get them out of here until they are exhausted… and you know how long that takes! This is a big American-style theme park with all the trimmings. Corkscrew, Bumping Cars, Swinging Viking Boat, Rollercoaster – they are all there, and more besides. The Fan Favorite is the Giants House where everything is 50 times as big as it should be – very convincing and it would be a great place to have a party. The Super Splash and White-Water Rapids are water rides and please note: You will get wet! Dream World also has a full-fledged go-kart track and ‘Snow Land’ – a display of artificial snow (although this attraction is not included in the main ticket fee). Dream World also features a live ‘Hollywood Action Show’ which up until recently  
had a dubious display of Nazi Storm Troopers successfully invading an Allied camp. Many people are glad that the current focus has changed to a SWAT team invading a criminal’s den. This is well worth the trip. If you have kids (and the time) take them. Don’t miss the extravaganza! Meet your favorite cartoon characters in the spectacular The Colors of the World Parade. Oh! Yes, Dreamy will be in the parade too. Round-trip transfer from the hotel is provided with this package for your comfort.  
Overnight in Bangkok
