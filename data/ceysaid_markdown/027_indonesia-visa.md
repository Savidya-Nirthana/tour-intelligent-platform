# Indonesia Visa

### **Required Documents for Visa Application**

1. **White Background Photo**
2. **Bank Balance Confirmation Letter**
   * A **confirmation letter** from your bank showing a **balance of USD 2000** (or the equivalent in Sri Lankan Rupees).
3. **Guest Telephone Number**
4. **Air Ticket**
5. **Hotel Reservation**

### **Visa Processing and Fees**

* **Processing Time**: Typically **5 working days**.
* **Visa Fee is Non-refundable**, even if the application is rejected.

### **Important Notes**

* Ensure all documents are complete and accurately filled out to avoid delays.
