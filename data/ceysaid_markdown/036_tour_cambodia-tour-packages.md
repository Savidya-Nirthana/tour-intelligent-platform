## 07 Days Cambodia Group Tour

Welcome to our Cambodia 07 days Group Tour 2024

**Day 01  PHNOM PENH ARRIVAL (D)**  
Upon arrival at Phnom Penh airport, greeted by our tour guide and driver transferred to hotel for check in. Room may not ready until 14:00 pm. This evening enjoys your dinner at the local restaurant.

Meal: Dinner.  
Accommodation: At hotel in Phnom Penh.

**Day 02  PHNOM PENH HALF DAY CITY TOUR (B, L, D)**  
Breakfast at hotel.  
Morning, visit to the Royal Palace, serves as the residence of the King, a venue for court ceremony and as a symbol of the Kingdom. Inside the Palace grounds street sounds are silenced by the high walls and the royal buildings. Attached to the Palace compound, Wat Preah Keo Morokat (Silver Pagoda) is unique.amongst pagodas, it is so named for its 5000 silver tiled floors.

Lunch at a local restaurant.

Afternoon, visit Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum (S21 prison). This prison was a high school, and used as a prison by Pol Pot’s security forces and became the largest center for detention and torture during the rule of the Khmer Rouge. Also, visit Russian Market (Psah Tuol Thom Pong), a lively outdoor market where you will find antiquities, silver and gold jewelry, gems, silk, kramas, stone and wood carvings, as well as TShirts, CDs and other souvenirs. Visit Wat Phnom Temple, a small hill crowned by an active Wat (Pagoda) marks the legendary founding place of the Phnom Penh. Next, we will take a short stop for photograph at Independent Monument. Then drive along Sothearos Blvd for city view and short stop for a walk to enjoy fresh air at Chaktomouk River Bank. Transfer back to hotel. Dinner at Iocal restaurant. Free and overnight in Phnom Penh.

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.  
Accommodation: At hotel in Phnom Penh.

**Day 03  PHNOM PENH – PRIVATE OVERLAND TRANSFER TO SIEM REAP – TONLE SAP (B, L, D)**  
Breakfast at hotel, then overland to Siem Reap (with guide). Driving distance take about 5-6 hours. You may have lunch break along the way or at Siem Reap. Upon arrival Siem Reap, transfer directly to hotel for check in and short rest. Start tour to Tonle Sap Lake and its floating village, Chong Kneas Floating Village and fishing community. You will learn about the unique ecological system of this lake which makes it one of the most productive lakes in Asia. Enjoy cruising around the floating village to observe the way of life of people living on the lake. If time still permit, continue to visit, Satcha Handicraft Center, the Cambodian handicraft center that incubates local artisans, mixing traditional knowledge with contemporary design to showcase Cambodian’ talents to local and international visitors and to transmit these know-how over time while having a sustainable social, economic, and environmental impact.

Enjoy dinner at local restaurant. Overnight at hotel in Siem Reap.

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.  
Accommodation: At hotel in Siem Reap.

**Day 04  ANGKOR WAT – ANGKOR THOM – TA PROHM (B, L, D)**  
After breakfast at the hotel, today visit to the antique capital of Angkor Thom. start from the South Gate with its huge statues depicting the churning of the ocean of milk, the Bayon Temple, unique for its 54 towers decorated with over 200 smiling faces of Avalokiteshvara, the Phimeanakas, the Royal Enclosure, the Elephants Terrace and the Terrace of the Leper King and Ba Poun Temple. Continue to visit jungle Ta Prohm Temple, embraced by the roots of enormous fig trees and gigantic creepers. Which is a wonderful combination of human genius and natural beauty! Giant trees have grown here for centuries. Lunch at local restaurant.

Afternoon at the hotel, today explore Angkor Wat Temple the most famous of all the temples on the plain of Angkor. The temple complex covers 81 hectares and is comparable in size to the Imperial Palace in Beijing. Its distinctive five towers emblazoned on the Cambodian flag and the 12th century masterpiece considered by art historians to be the prime example of classical Khmer art and architecture. Evening Enjoy stunning sunset views on the top of Ba Kheng Mountain.

Enjoy dinner at local restaurant. Overnight at hotel in Siem Reap.

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.  
Accommodation: At hotel in Siem Reap.

**Day 05  BANTEAY SREI + CITY TOUR (B, L, D)**  
After breakfast, we will drive off the city to the enchanting temple of the Banteay Srei, nearly everyone’s favorite site. The special charm of this temple lies in its compact size, remarkable state of preservation, and the excellence of it decorative carvings. Make a short stop at Pradak Village, where you will see the villagers making palm sugar, handicraft for selling to tourist. It’s a nice chance for you to see the local people living at the country side with their struggling for daily life. Lunch at local restaurant. Afternoon back to the city, we will explore Siem Reap City, included Preah Ang Check Preah Ang Chorm, where local people pay the respect and pray for good luck when they visit Siem Reap. Continue to visit, Satcha Handicraft Center, the Cambodian handicraft center that incubates local artisans, mixing traditional knowledge with contemporary design to showcase Cambodian’ talents to local and international visitors and to transmit these know-how over time while having a sustainable social, economic, and environmental impact. Last stop of the day at Old market, the busiest market in town where most tourist comes for some souvenirs.

Enjoy dinner at local restaurant.  
Overnight at hotel in Siem Reap.

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.  
Accommodation: At hotel in Siem Reap.

**Day 06  SIEM REAP – PRIVATE OVERLAND TRANSFER TO PHNOM PENH (B, L, D)**  
Breakfast at hotel, then overland transfer to Phnom Penh (with guide). Driving distance take about 5-6 hours. You may have lunch at Phnom Penh at local restaurants. Upon arrival Phnom Penh, transfer directly to hotel for check in and rest. Enjoy your free day in Phnom Penh. This evening enjoy your dinner at local restaurant. Overnight at hotel in Phnom Penh.

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.  
Accommodation: At hotel in Phnom Penh.

**Day 07  PHNOM PENH (B)**  
Breakfast at hotel, free and transfer to Phnom Penh Airport for departure.  
Meal: Breakfast
