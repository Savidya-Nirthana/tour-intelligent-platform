**Day 02 Istanbul (SIC basis) Breakfast + Lunch**  
After breakfast, we will explore this beautiful city passing by Istanbul City Walls, massive walls of Ancient Constantinople. Then visit the Blue Mosque which boasts a unique feature of 6 minarets. Then, proceed to the only remains of Hippodrome of Constantinople, which consists of 3 monumental columns and a fountain. Visit Topkapi Palace, it is a museum palace with a wide collection of porcelain, antiques, holy relics and other treasures. Next proceed to Hagia Sophia (from outside), once a church but converted into a mosque, one of the greatest marvels of architecture. At the end of tour, we proceed to the Grand Bazaar for shopping.  
Lunch at local restaurant  
Overnight in Istanbul.

**Day 03 Istanbul (SIC basis) Hotel Breakfast + Lunch**  
After a delicious breakfast you will leave for the famous Princes’ Islands, which is one of the favourite local spots,to beat the monotony of city life and venturing into an entirely different, vibrant atmosphere. The lavishly decorated Ottoman mansions and Victorian themed villas will leave you spellbound. After you have explored the island, enjoy a delectable lunch at a seaside restaurant. You will return from the hotel via ferry and will be transferred back to the hotel.  
Lunch at local restaurant  
Overnight in Istanbul.

Free day
