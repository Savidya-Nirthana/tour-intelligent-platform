# Cambodia Visa

#### **Cambodia Visa Required Documents:**

1. **Passport Copy**
   * A clear photocopy of your passport’s data page.
2. **Photograph**
   * A **white background photo** (passport-size).
3. **Hotel Reservation**
4. **Air Tickets**
5. **Telephone Number**

#### **e-Visa Details:**

* **e-Visa Type**: **Tourist (T)**
* **Visa Fee**: **Non-refundable**
