**Day 01: Colombo – Delhi (by Flight) Delhi - Varanasi (by Flight) {Lunch/Dinner)**  
Arrive Delhi airport and connect domestic flight to Varanasi. Arrive Varanasi Airport. Meet and assistance on arrival and transfer to hotel for check in. After Lunch visit Sarnath along with Pirith Ceremony (where Lord Buddha delivered his first sermon). Also visit Sarnath Museum and Stupa. Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

**Day 02: Varanasi – Bodhgaya (255 Kms/06 hrs) (Breakfast / Lunch/ Dinner)**  
After breakfast drive to Bodhgaya. On arrival check in at hotel. After Lunch at visit Mahabodhi Temple. Dinner and overnight stay at Hotel in Bodhgaya

**Day 03: Bodhgaya (Breakfast / Lunch/ Dinner)**  
Morning visit Mahabodhi Temple. Offer Milk Rice to Bodhi Tree and Observe SIL. Evening sight scene at Niranjana River, Sujata Temple - which is across the Niranjana river' it's 30 minutes walk,Then visit Dungeshwari Caves. Dinner and overnight stay at  
Hotel.  
**Day 04: Bodhgaya – Rajgir (Nalanda) (90 Kms/02 hrs) (Breakfast / Lunch/Dinner)**  
Early Morning offer Heel Danaya to Sri Lanka Monks at Mahabodhi Society. After breakfast drive to Rajgir.Visit Gijjakuta (Vulture Hill), hot spring in Rajgir, World Peace Stupa, Valuvana (Bamboo's Garden). After lunch at hotel visit Nalanda to see ruins of  
world oldest University.Dinner and overnight stay at hotel Rajgir.

**Day 05: Rajgir – Vaishali (150 Kms/04 hrs)**  
**Vaishali  - Kushinagar (175 Kms/05 hrs) {Breakfast / Lunch/ Dinner)**

After breakfast, drive to Kushinagar. Enroute visit Vaishali Stupa and Ashoka Pillar, Hot Lunch at Vaishali. After Lunch, drive to Kushinagar on arrival Kushinagar transfer to the Hotel. Dinner and overnight stay at Hotel in Kushinagar

**Day 06: Kushinagar – Lumbini (180 Kms/05 hrs){Breakfast / Lunch/ Dinner)**  
Early morning visit Maha Parinirwana Temple (where Buddha took his last breathe) and Rambhar Stupa (cremation site of Lord Buddha) Japan Temple. After breakfast, drive to Lumbini (Nepal). While going to Lumbini, stop at India/Nepal border for Visa/Immigration formalities. Later check-in at Hotel. 15 Min. drive from the Nepal Border. After lunch, visit Lumbini, the Birthplace of Lord Buddha. Visit Maya Devi Temple and Ashokan Pillar. Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

**Day 07: Lumbini – Sravasti (225 Kms/05 hrs) {Breakfast / Lunch/ Dinner)**  
After breakfast, drive to Sravasti. On arrival check in at hotel. After lunch visit Sravasti, where Buddha spent his 24 monsoons, visit Jetvana and Ananda Bodhi Tree &amp; Attend to the Bodhi Pooja. Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

**Day 08: Sravasti – Lucknow (170 Kms/03-4 hrs) (Breakfast)Lucknow – Delhi (by Domestic Flight) Delhi – Colombo (by Flight)**  
After breakfast, drive to Lucknow and straight transfer to airport to board the flight to Delhi to connect flight to Colombo.
