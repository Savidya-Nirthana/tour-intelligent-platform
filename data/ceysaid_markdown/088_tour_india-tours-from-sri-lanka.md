## Kerala Back Water Tour with Houseboat

**Day 01: Arrive Cochin (by Flight) (No Meal)**  
Arrive Cochin, meet and assistance on arrival and transfer to hotel for check in. Overnight stay at hotel.

**Day 02: Cochin – Munnar (150 Kms/05 hrs)(Breakfast)**  
After breakfast full day city tour of Cochin, visit to the Dutch Palace, Jewish Synagogue, St. Francis Church,  
Chinese Fishing nets.  
Later drive to Munnar. On arrival check in at hotel. Overnight stay at hotel.

**Day 03: Munnar (Breakfast)**  
A morning sightseeing trip of Munnar; visiting the Eravikulam National Park where you can see the very  
rare mountain goats locally called the Nilgiri Tahr, Visit Mattupetty Dam, Kundala Dam, Echo Point, View  
Point Tea Gardens. Rest of the day at leisure and enjoy natural beauty. Overnight stay at hotel.

**Day 04: Munnar – Alleppey (168 Kms/04 hrs)(Breakfast/ Lunch/ Dinner)**  
After breakfast proceed to Alleppey, 168 kms and 04 hrs. Check in to the waiting houseboat. Enroute you  
can see many romantic beauty of the nature which will remind you that you are iGod's Own  
Country : You can view coconut palm fringed canals and local people going for a fresh catch of the day  
Tapping of toddy (Local beverages) from palm trees. Rest of the day at leisure. Lunch, dinner and  
overnight stay at the houseboat.

**Day 05: Alleppey – Cochin Departure (90 Kms/02 hrs) (Breakfast)**  
After breakfast drive to Cochin and straight transfer to airport to board the flight for onward journey.

\*\*\*END OF THE TOUR \*\*\*
