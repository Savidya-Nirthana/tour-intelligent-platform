**DAY 01 Colombo – Cochin (by Flight) Cochin – Alleppey (78 Kms/02 hrs) Lunch / Dinner**

Arrive Cochin airport, meet and assistance on arrival and drive to Alleppey. On arrival check in at Houseboat. Locally known as Kettuvallam. Experience the serenity of Kerala backwater on a luxury Alleppey houseboat, equipped with modern amenities and different kinds of rooms. This is the most cheering part of your Kerala honeymoon tour packages.

Sailing across Kerala backwaters on a Houseboat is one of the most cherished dreams for the honeymooners. Enjoy the sight of paddy fields, palm-fringed lakes, birds, and sunset. Then, savor some toddy (palm wine) on your way to add an authentic touch to your journey. This is followed by an overnight stay on the Alleppey houseboat.

**DAY 02 Alleppey – Cochin (58 Kms/ 1.5 hrs) Breakfast / Dinner**  
Today after breakfast drive to Cochin. On arrival check in at hotel. Later visit St. Francis Church, Chinese fishing  
nets, Dutch palace & Jewish synagogue. Overnight stay at hotel.

**DAY 03 Cochin – Chennai Departure (by Flight) Chennai – Pondicherry (150 Kms/ 03 hrs) Breakfast / Dinner**

Today after breakfast on time departure transfer to Cochin airport to board the flight to Chennai. Arrive Chennai and drive to Pondicherry. On arrival check in at hotel.

**DAY 04 Pondicherry Breakfast / Dinner**

Today after breakfast full day city tour of Pondicherry covering visit French Colony, Ashram, Manakulavinayakar temple, beach & Auroville. Overnight stay at hotel.

**DAY 05 Pondicherry – Chennai (150 Kms/ 03 hrs) Breakfast / Dinner**

Today after breakfast drive to Chennai. On arrival check in at hotel. Later visit Kapileshwar Temple, Santhom Church and Marina Beach. Rest of the time free for shopping at T Nagar shopping area. Overnight stay at hotel.

**DAY 06 Chennai – Colombo Departure (by Flight) Breakfast**

Today after breakfast on time departure transfer to airport to board the flight to Colombo
