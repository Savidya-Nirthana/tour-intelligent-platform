# Laos Visa

### **Visa Requirements for Travelers**

To apply for a **Laos Visa**, applicants must provide the following documents:

1. **Passport Copy**
2. **Photograph**
3. **Hotel Reservation**
4. **Flight Ticket**
5. **Contact Information**

### **Visa Processing & Fees**

* **e-Visa (Tourist Visa – T): USD 50+** (excluding bank charges).
* **Visa fees are non-refundable**, even if the application is rejected.

### **Important Notes**

* Travelers must ensure all **required documents are submitted correctly**.
* e-Visa processing times may vary, and additional documents may be requested if necessary.
